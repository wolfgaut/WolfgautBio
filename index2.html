License

A Lot of my music is licensed under this license:
Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)

In short, this means that you can use my music for anything as long as you give credit. Credit must include my name Wolfgaut and the title of the song. If possible, provide a link to the song on my SoundCloud or YouTube.

Example
If you want to use my song IDK2.Flac in your YouTube video, include this snippet of text in the video description box:

Wolfgaut- IDK2.Flac
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0q5PR1xpA0
CC BY-SA 4.0

Warning Not all my song is under this license

Download

All my music is available for both streaming and download on SoundCloud. Click the More button underneath the waveform and a drop-down menu will reveal the download option. You can also stream my music on YouTube.
